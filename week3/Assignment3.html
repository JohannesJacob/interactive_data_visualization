Trump Family Tree

Assignment 3: Johannes Jacob, Mona Timmermann, Tobias Ludwig

The following visualisation shows a family tree created for the Trump family based on this data.
The goal was to create an easy-to-read tree. We have made the following decisions:

  • Partners have been put on their own level for simplification
  • Links between people's names as basic easy-to-read lines
  • Differentiation between Husband/Wife and Parent/Child relationship by color
  • Legend to explain meaning of colours

Possible improvements could be:
  • Having partners on a subordinated level can be confusing at first. Something similar to this might be nicer.
  • The visualisation seems too wide and should probably be narrowed a little.
  • Adding more information, e.g.:
    1. photo of corresponding person
    2. current relationship status (married, divorced)
    3. (age and/or birthday)
    4. current job
    5. place of residence
  • The information mentioned above could nicely be implemented as pop-up windows / switching through idioms
