Ideas for projects

Tobi

Horizon 2020 is a project of the EU for funding research, development and innovation in the range of 2014-2020.

  1. How is this money distributed over countries, who profits the most? How many proposals are accepted/rejected?
  2. Data is available at the official EU website, however their visualizations are a bit overwhelming.
  3. Data could visualized by a heat-colored map of Europe, interactive elements would be a slider for time and extra-details on countries that pop up when hovering over them.
  4. Audience could be students who are considering to move to a European country for doing their PhD or the like.

Johannes

Recently I worked on a project examining the influence of UN peacekeeping missions on refugee flows to Europe. As this was only a strict quantitative analyis the reader could only rely on results from regressions depicted in tables. In a visulization project I would like to implement the following features:

  • Map refugees flows from African conflict areas to Europe. Preferbly in a stream kind of manner.
  • Add an interactive bar that allows the user to change the number of UN personnel deployed to a conflict and see the reactions in the refugee streams.
  • Add circles with the number of deaths in a conflict such that the spectator is able to sense the conflict brutality by the size of the circle.

All the data is available online at the download site of the Uppsala Conflict Data Program.
